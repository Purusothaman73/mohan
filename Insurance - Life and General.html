Life and General Insurance from Mohan Finserv

Are you looking to secure your financial future and protect your loved ones while taking out a home loan? At Mohan Finserv, we understand that home loans come with long-term commitments, and having adequate insurance coverage can provide you with peace of mind. We offer both Life Insurance and General Insurance as part of our value-added services, ensuring that you are financially safeguarded at every step of your journey.

Life Insurance Process and Procedure

Consultation and Risk Assessment:
The first step is to evaluate your financial situation, loan amount, and the number of dependents. Our insurance experts at Mohan Finserv will conduct a risk assessment to determine the ideal life insurance coverage you need. This ensures that your family’s financial future remains secure, even if the worst were to happen.
Selection of Insurance Plans:
Based on your requirements, we will present you with various life insurance options, including term life policies, whole life insurance, or home loan-linked insurance. These policies are designed to provide a lump sum payout to your beneficiaries in the event of your demise, ensuring that the home loan is paid off and your family is financially protected.
Application and Documentation:
To proceed with life insurance, you will need to complete an application form and provide essential documents like identity proof, age proof, medical history, and details of your home loan. Depending on the policy, a medical examination may also be required, especially if the loan amount is high or if you are above a certain age.
Policy Issuance and Premium Payment:
After evaluating your application and documents, Mohan Finserv will help you finalize your life insurance policy. Once your policy is issued, you will be required to pay the premium either as a lump sum or via monthly/quarterly installments, based on your preference.
Policy Activation and Coverage:
After the premium is paid, the insurance policy becomes active, and your home loan will be covered. You will receive regular updates on your policy’s status and renewal reminders.

General Insurance Process and Procedure

Consultation and Requirement Understanding:
We begin by understanding your specific requirements, such as home, health, or vehicle insurance. If you’re taking a home loan, property insurance becomes essential to safeguard your asset against natural calamities, fire, theft, or other potential risks. Our team will assess your needs and help you choose the most suitable coverage.
Policy Selection:
Mohan Finserv provides a variety of general insurance products, including:

  • Home Insurance: Covers damages or loss to your property caused by natural disasters, fire, or theft.
  • Health Insurance: Provides coverage for medical emergencies, hospitalization, and treatment costs.
  • Vehicle Insurance: Offers protection against damages or theft of your vehicle.
  • Personal Accident Insurance: Provides coverage in case of accidents that lead to disability or death.
  • Property Insurance: Ensures your real estate investment is protected against unforeseen risks.
    • Application and Documentation:
    Once you’ve selected the appropriate insurance plan, you will need to submit required documents like proof of property ownership, medical history (for health insurance), or vehicle details (for vehicle insurance). We help you gather and submit all the necessary paperwork to make the process smoother.
    Policy Activation:
    After premium payment, your general insurance policy becomes active, offering coverage for the specified risks. You will receive your policy document, which includes all the terms and conditions of coverage, and we’ll keep you updated on policy renewals.
    Claim Assistance:
    In the event of a claim, our team will assist you in navigating the claim process. Whether it’s for property damage, medical treatment, or other scenarios, Mohan Finserv ensures that you’re supported every step of the way. We help you file claims, coordinate with insurance providers, and ensure a timely settlement.
