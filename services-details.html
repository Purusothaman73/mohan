Loans for New Flat Purchase from Mohan Finserv

Are you dreaming of owning a brand-new flat in Chennai but need financial assistance to make it happen? Mohan Finserv is here to guide you through every step of the loan process, making it simple and stress-free. Our new flat purchase loans come with competitive interest rates, flexible repayment options, and personalized assistance.

Loan Process and Procedure for New Flat Purchase

Initial Consultation:
The process begins with a consultation to understand your financial requirements, eligibility, and preferred loan amount. This stage involves discussing your income, current liabilities, and credit score.
Application Submission:
Once you’ve chosen the loan plan that suits you best, submit a completed loan application form along with the required documents, which typically include identity proof, address proof, income statements, bank statements, and property details.
Documentation and Verification:
Our team at Mohan Finserv will assist you in compiling all necessary documents. This stage involves verification checks to confirm your income, employment details, and property documents.
Loan Approval:
After the documentation process, the loan application is evaluated and approved based on eligibility and creditworthiness. This is followed by a sanction letter detailing the approved loan amount, interest rate, and other terms.
Property Valuation and Legal Check:
We conduct a thorough legal and technical check of the property to ensure clear ownership and market valuation. This step ensures that your investment is secure.
Loan Agreement and Signing:
Post approval, you’ll be asked to sign the loan agreement. This agreement outlines the terms, repayment schedule, and other conditions of the loan.
Disbursement of Funds:
Once all formalities are complete, the approved loan amount is disbursed directly to the seller or developer. At Mohan Finserv, we ensure a quick disbursal so you can take possession of your new flat without delay.
